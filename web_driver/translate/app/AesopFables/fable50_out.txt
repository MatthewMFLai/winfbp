Fable 50

The Eagle(鷹) and the Lion as Partners

An eagle flew up to a lion and asked him to be his partner(搭檔，夥伴). 'I don't see why not,' replied(回答) the lion. 'But first you must give me your long wing-feathers as a pledge(保證) that you will keep your promise(允諾，答應). How will I be able to trust(相信) you as a friend if you do not stay here with me?'


eagle 鷹 
partner 搭檔，夥伴，同伴 
pledge 誓言；諾言；保證 
promise 允諾，答應；保證 
replied 回答，答覆 
trust 信任，信賴，相信
